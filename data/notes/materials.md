# Materials

- [LearnCpp](https://www.learncpp.com/) - main course.
- [cppreference](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/language.html) - exact language and library details.
- [Exercism C++ track](https://exercism.org/tracks/cpp) - practice and repetition.
- [C++ Core Guidelines](https://isocpp.github.io/CppCoreGuidelines/CppCoreGuidelines) - style and good practices, later.
