Hi, I’m Hitsuji, the creator and maintainer of NextPaper.













NextPaper is my Next.js take on a minimal, readable blog theme inspired by [AstroPaper](https://github.com/satnaing/astro-paper). It keeps the practical publishing setup from [Tailwind Nextjs Starter Blog](https://github.com/timlrx/tailwind-nextjs-starter-blog), but reshapes the interface into something simpler and calmer.

It is built around the things I actually want in a personal blog: MDX, Contentlayer, tags, archives, local search, comments, RSS and sitemap.

You can find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/Hitsujii), follow me on [X](https://x.com/neggora), or reach me by email at [dev.hitsuji@gmail.com](mailto:dev.hitsuji@gmail.com).
